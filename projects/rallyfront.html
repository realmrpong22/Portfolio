Rallyfront

Hybrid Tower Defense • Unity • C#

Overview

Rallyfront is a hybrid tower defense game that combines traditional defensive gameplay with direct player involvement. In addition to placing and managing towers, players can actively move around the map to repair defenses and support their setup during combat.

The project focuses on blending strategic planning with real-time decision-making, requiring players to balance long-term tower placement with immediate in-game actions.

Gameplay

  • Classic tower defense structure with enemy waves
  • Player-controlled character that can move and repair towers
  • Resource management for building and maintaining defenses
  • Increasing difficulty through enemy scaling
  • Strategic positioning combined with active player intervention

Engine

Unity 2022.3.62f2

Language

C#

Role

Game Designer

Design Breakdown

The core design goal was to enhance traditional tower defense gameplay by introducing player agency during combat. Instead of relying solely on automated defenses, players must actively respond to threats by repairing damaged towers and reinforcing weak points.

This hybrid approach creates a dynamic gameplay loop where strategic preparation and real-time decision-making are equally important, increasing engagement compared to passive tower defense systems.

Systems Design

  • Designed and balanced multiple tower types with distinct roles
  • Implemented damage, attack rate, and range tuning for gameplay balance
  • Ensured each tower contributes meaningfully to different strategies
  • Balanced progression to maintain challenge across waves

Gameplay Balance

  • Iterated on tower efficiency and cost to achieve fair difficulty scaling
  • Adjusted enemy pressure to encourage active player intervention
  • Tested different configurations to support multiple viable playstyles
