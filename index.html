BUV Game Jam 2025

The One After

48-Hour Game Jam • May 19-21, 2025
Team of 3 • Gameplay, UI & Enemy Designer
Turn-based, Boss Rush • Unity

Theme: "Only One"

Turn-based 1v1 boss battle featuring a QTE-style dodging system that adds real-time reaction to strategic planning. The player faces a single, powerful enemy — embodying the theme "Only One." Failure isn’t the end — it means passing your knowledge and strength to the next challenger, reinforcing a legacy of growth through defeat. Until one can stand tall against the powerful enemy.

I designed and animated the boss, implemented attack patterns, and built the main menu and win screen. I worked with a team of 2 other students, and through the experience I learned how to fast prototyping and collaborate under pressure.
